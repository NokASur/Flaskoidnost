About levels...


There are 5 levels of physical strength distinguished on our site. We determine your level after you completed your first test

Each level consists of progressively harder tasks and exercises with higher consistency.

It is advised to take a test every few weeks in order to keep difficulty of your tasks in line with your actual possibilities

Level descriptions

First level (1) - You are just starting out, and you need to actively try your muscles in action

Second Level (2) - You have some experience with basic exercises and starting to increase your limits

Second Level (3) - You have achieved a decent level already, but some specific activities can still be hard for you

Second Level (4) - You are close to mastering out your stamina and power

Second Level (5) - You are a guru of sport and actively seeking out for our toughest challenges
